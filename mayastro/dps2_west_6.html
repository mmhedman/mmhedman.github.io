Overall Summary: On [4 Ak'bal 11 Muwan (9.12.0.8.3)], war befell Dragon Water, it was done by Nuun Ujol Chaak. B'alah Chan K'awiil, the Holy Lord of Mutul, escaped and went up to Chak Nah.
