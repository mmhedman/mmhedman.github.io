A Perfectly Sensible Scientific Alphabet


A is for Vector Potential
B is for Magnetic Field
c is for the Speed of Light
D is for Aspartic Acid
E is for Glutamic Acid
f is for True Anomaly
g is for the Metric Tensor
h is for Planck's Constant
I is for Current
j is also for Current
k is for Wavenumber
L is for Inductance
M is for Radio Waves between 60 and 100 GHz
n is for Refractive Index
O is for Pyrrolysine
p is for Momentum
Q is for Electric Charge
R is for the Gas Constant
S is for Entropy
T is for Kinetic Energy
U is for Potential Energy
v is for Speed
W is for the Number of States
X is for Reactance
Y is for Admittance
Z is for Metallicity
